First feedback

Simon Potocnak

14.09.2022

Before beginning my study I was asked to fill in the form that will provide teachers with some basic information about who am I, hence the name of the form was Who am I.

I was supposed to write about why I choose this study program and explain my choice. Then I should explain what I want to do after completing this program. The next thing they asked me to do was personal SWAT analysis as well as my extra-curricular activities and some other things.

After completing and submitting the form I waited for about 2 weeks and Mrs. Daphne provided feedback on what I have written.

The feedback itself was quite expansive and detailed which I appreciated because I was able to improve my answers. And make them better. It also made me feel a little bit insecure, because I am not use to open myself and write neither about my strengths or weaknesses nor about what I want to do after completing something.

In the conclusion the feedback itself was more than appreciated ;from my side, but it felt little bit weird. Therefore I need to improve at opening myself.
